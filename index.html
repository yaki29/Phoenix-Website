ABOUT US

PHOENIX CLUB is not just a club - it is a hub where all the potential minds of LNMIIT gather to bring something dynamic , a place where 2D Things metamorphosize into 3- dimensional reality, with a view of catering the needs of students by giving opportunities to learn by doing things practically .

The club today is an active platform for students to display and develop their practical machine building skills and knowledge.

We believe that learning outside the classroom can be as formative as what happens inside the classroom. The Club offers indispensible guidance, events , classes and tutorials along with components , workspace and tools .

We pour in our every ounce of energy and support to satisfy your curiosity. The people at phoenix are technology enthusiasts and foster skills in technology and its advancement within the community.

It has been our constant endeavor to increase the participation of students in this club and help them gain some technical knowledge and its practical application.

We firmly believe in working in a planned, organized and disciplined manner that helps us in risk free management.

to Publicize LNMIIT to expand further by organizing various events at college as well as national level

TORQUE

Its like a basic Robo race in which a manual controlled robot need to cover a path contain obstacles like rope bridge, rocky terrain, zig-zag, domino, bumpers etc. in the minimum time. The points will be given on the obstacles cleared. There will be some time bonus also.

MAZE SOLVER

The way the Micromouse Robot Competition works is a two-stage process. The robots first enter the maze and have a chance to map it out. They then get a second timed run where the object is to solve the maze as quickly as possible.

Amphibian

The team has to make a manually controlled robot travels on varied land terrains and also water surfaces. The gameplay includes competing a certain task in minimum time.

Wire Junkies

This event is much like lab test. A quiz is given to participants in the first round. A certain teams will be selected for second round which are given problem statement much like lab assignment.

Process It

The problem statement is mainly based on Interpolation.
It test the persons skill to code in MATLAB and make desired changes to the image.

Junkyard Wars

This event is fun to be a part of.
This is conducted in 2 rounds, where in the first round some teams are selected based on an Electronics and Data Interpretation quiz, while in the second round the Teams are to constuct a contraption given in the statement from Junk, which is provided to them.
